# uk-renewable-energy-analysis
UK Energy Transition: Analysing the Shift from Fossil Fuels to Renewables (1990–2020).
This project analyses UK energy consumption data from 1990 to 2020, examining the shift from fossil fuels to renewable sources across 30 years of national energy records.

The following questions are to be answered using the dataset:

•At what point did renewable consumption exceed a meaningful threshold relative to fossil fuels, and how long did it take to get there from near-zero?

•Which renewable sources are driving the growth; is it one dominant type pulling the total up, or is growth spread across multiple sources?

•Are there years where fossil fuel consumption dropped sharply, and do those years correspond to renewable growth or something else entirely (recession, policy change, price spike)?
